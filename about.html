Our Story

Welcome to Layviiii

A Culinary Journey Built on Passion and Tradition

Founded in 2018, Layviiii began as a vision to elevate premium dining by pairing flawless, slow-smoked authentic barbecue techniques and artisan wood-fired culinary arts with strict 100% Halal sourcing. Every dish tells a story of patience, precise temperature control, and deep culinary respect.

What started as an exclusive single-smoker kitchen has grown into a destination layout for food enthusiasts who appreciate fine textures, from our standard-setting 12-hour wood-fired Wagyu brisket to delicate mocktail mixology.

Our Mission

To redefine the parameters of upscale dining by delivering unparalleled, handcrafted Halal menus without ever compromising on authenticity, pristine presentation, or culinary innovation.

Our Vision

To set the global baseline for premium culinary brands, creating a space where traditional wood-fired methodology seamlessly merges with modern architectural dining experiences.
